Advanced search

Description:
  1. Go to the form of advanced search on the left.
  2. Select the form fields that help you to display your target books. Note: You can left blanc some fields.
  3. Once you fill in the form, you may sumbit it and books according to your search will be displayed.

Rapid search

Description:
  1. Go to Search Input  in the application.
  2. Select the book that you want to purchase.
    Note:  You can search by name, by category, by author or by edition of the book.
  3. Once you click on the icon of research, the chosen book will be displayed.

Add to cart

Description:
  1. Go to the catalog of books.
  2. Select the book that you want to add in your cart.
    Note:  You click on the 'shop button' at the buttom of the chosen book.

Pass command

Description:
  1. Click on the icon of cart in the menu.
  2. Books that are added in the cart will be showed.
  3. You may choose the quantity of books that you want to purchase.
  4. Click to 'Pass to command'.
    Note:  The command will be confirmed only if you are signed in.
  5. If you are connected, you will be redirected to fill a form which contains your shipping information :
    First Name
    Last Name
    Address
    State
    Country
    Credit number
